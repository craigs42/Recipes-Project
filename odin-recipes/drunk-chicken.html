Drunk Chicken

Description

This is a beautiful traditional Japanese dish with loads of flavor.

Wonderfully convenient, simple in its ingredients, short cook time, and loaded with flavor. Read on for the details!

Ingredients

  • 700 ml shaoxing wine
  • 250 ml mirin
  • 250 ml water
  • 40g jaggery/palm sugar
  • 5 cm ginger sliced
  • 3 skin on chicken breast fillets (approx 250g)

Instructions

  1. Remove skin from chicken, carefully scrape any excess fat from the underside of the skin and place onto on a lined tray. Sprinkle with salt and cover with baking paper then another tray. Bake in oven until crispy, check after 20 minutes, may need a little longer. Break into shards and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan add the shaoxing wine, mirin, water, jaggery and ginger. Bring to the boil. Add chicken breasts then remove from heat. Cover with a lid and allow to poach for 20 minutes.
  3. When cool to touch, shred chicken breasts, reserving the poaching liquid.
